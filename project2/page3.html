Korean Bulgogi

About Bulgogi

Bulgogi is a Korean dish made from thin slices of beef marinated in a sweet and savory mixture, then cooked quickly to keep the meat tender. It is often served with rice, lettuce wraps, and banchan (side dishes).

Ingredients

  • 1 lb thinly sliced beef (ribeye or sirloin)
  • 4 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp sugar or honey
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small pear or apple, grated (optional tenderizer)
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Recipe Steps

  1. Make the marinade by mixing soy sauce, sugar or honey, sesame oil, garlic, grated fruit, and ginger.
  2. Add the sliced beef and green onions. Mix well and marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Heat a pan on medium-high and cook the beef in batches until browned and cooked through.
  4. Garnish with sesame seeds and serve with rice or in lettuce wraps.
